Gameplay

Simple to learn.
Impossible to master.

Guide the ball through an endless stream of obstacles. Every tap counts. One wrong move and it's game over.

1

Tap to Move

Tap the screen to start the ball moving. It's that simple to begin.

2

Tap to Switch

Tap again to change direction. Left or right -- timing is everything.

3

Hit the Gaps

Aim for the colored gaps in each line. Pass through to score points.

4

Don't Crash

Avoid walls and solid lines. As your score rises, the challenge intensifies.
